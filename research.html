Research

arXiv ORCID
I am interested in differential and complex geometry.
Some specific topics are: Complex Monge-Ampère Equations, Kaehler-Einstein metrics, Kaehler-Ricci Flow, Chern-Ricci Flow, Pluripotential Theory.
